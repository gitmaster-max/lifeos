Colour your plate — micronutrients

Macros fill the plate; micros decide how you feel. Rotate these through the week and your plates cover nearly everything.

What the plate can't carry — supplements

Vitamin B12

MANDATORY

No plate can carry it. 50 mcg daily or 1,000 mcg twice a week (cyanocobalamin). Your health module already flags yours at 42%.

Vitamin D

MANDATORY*

2,000 IU daily with a fat-containing meal — or 10 minutes of sun with 60% skin exposed. *Skip only if you truly get daily sun.

Protein powder · FITNESS

Tops up your 175 g daily target when plates fall short. Max 30 g per shake, within 30–60 min post-workout. Pea + rice blend gives a complete amino profile.

Creatine · FITNESS

3–5 g daily, any time — consistency beats timing. Strength, recovery and cognition; especially useful on plant-based diets. Not a steroid, no cycling needed.

Supplement minerals only when bloodwork shows a deficiency — upload your lab report in Health and LifeOS will flag what's low.
