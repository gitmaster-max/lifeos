Questions

How does LifeOS make money?

Through partner referrals — when a suggestion genuinely fits your situation and you take it, the partner pays us a fee. No ads, and your data is never sold or shared with partners.

What is a family account?

Up to 6 members share one LifeOS. Nominee assignments, document locations, premium reminders and health schedules are visible to the family — so nothing important stays unknown.

What does the DigiLocker connection do?

DigiLocker is the Government of India's document locker. LifeOS links to it so Aadhaar, PAN, licences and issued certificates appear in your family vault — LifeOS stores references, never the documents themselves.

Is my data safe?

In this preview, everything stays in your browser — nothing leaves your device. The product version will be encrypted and DPDP-compliant from day one.
